# 模块③ 构图（Composition）
> 核心问题：**观众的眼睛应该先看哪里，再看哪里，最后停在哪里？**
> 构图控制的是「视线的流动路径」和「画面的重量平衡」。

---

## 构图三要素（必须全部声明）

不指定构图时，AI 会默认生成「主体居中、背景虚化」的证件照式构图——平庸、无叙事感、视线一眼到底无处游走。

---

### 要素一：主体位置（打破居中陷阱）

**三分法（最常用）**
将画面横竖各三等分，主体放在交叉点或1/3线上：
```
Subject positioned at the right third of the frame
Subject positioned at the left third of the frame, 
  leaving right two-thirds as negative space
Subject placed at the lower-left intersection of the rule-of-thirds grid
```

**负空间（Negative Space）**
主体偏向一侧，另一侧大面积留白，暗示主体「看向哪里」或「走向哪里」：
```
Vast negative space to the [left/right], subject occupying only the [right/left] third
Subject dwarfed by overwhelming negative space above
```

**对称构图（谨慎使用）**
仅用于刻意强调秩序感、庄重感、韦斯·安德森风格：
```
Perfectly symmetrical center composition
Dead-center framing with bilateral symmetry
```

---

### 要素二：视觉动线（引导眼睛流动）

视觉动线决定观众「第二眼、第三眼看哪里」，没有动线的画面让人视觉疲劳。

**引导线（Leading Lines）**
用画面中的线条元素把视线引向主体或终点：
```
Leading lines created by [走廊/铁轨/公路/栏杆] directing the eye toward [主体/远方]
Receding corridor with repeating arched doorways creating strong leading lines toward a vanishing point
Street lights forming a converging line pulling the eye deep into the frame
```

**框中框（Frame within Frame）**
用场景元素自然框住主体，形成层次感：
```
Natural frame-within-frame created by [门框/窗框/树枝/幕布/拱门]
Subject framed by the gap between two massive [curtains/pillars/walls]
```

**对角线构图**
斜向引导线，增加动感和张力：
```
Diagonal composition pulling the eye from the lower-left foreground to the upper-right subject
Strong diagonal shadow cutting across the frame
```

**消失点透视**
强烈的纵深感，视线被吸入画面深处：
```
One-point perspective with strong vanishing point at center
Extreme depth perspective with [建筑/走廊/公路] receding to a single vanishing point
```

---

### 要素三：视觉重量平衡（非对称平衡）

画面左右/上下的「重量感」需要平衡，否则画面会让人感到倾斜不适。

**重量来源**：
- 亮的区域 > 暗的区域（亮处更吸眼）
- 暖色 > 冷色（暖色视觉重量更重）
- 大物体 > 小物体
- 清晰区域 > 模糊区域

**平衡写法**：
```
[主体] on the left, counterbalanced by [亮色光源/明亮物体] on the right
Heavy dark subject on the right, balanced by a bright practical light source on the left
Out-of-focus foreground element on the right providing visual mass to balance the sharp subject on the left
The dark figure on the left is visually counterweighted by the glowing window on the right
```

## 完整构图示例

**示例A：三分法 + 引导线 + 光源平衡**
```
Subject positioned at the right third of the frame.
Leading lines created by the wet cobblestone street
directing the eye from the lower-left toward the subject.
The dark subject on the right counterbalanced by 
a glowing neon sign anchoring the left side.
```

**示例B：框中框 + 中心对称 + 幕布平衡**
```
Perfectly symmetrical center composition.
Natural frame-within-frame created by two massive
golden satin curtains on both sides.
Deep stage blackness on both outer edges provides
visual weight anchoring the golden center.
```

**示例C：负空间 + 对角线 + 渺小感**
```
Tiny subject positioned at the lower-right intersection,
leaving vast negative space of stormy sky dominating
the upper-left three-quarters of the frame.
Diagonal horizon line pulling from lower-right to upper-left,
emphasizing the subject's smallness against the landscape.
```

---

## ⚠️ 常见错误

```
❌ 只写景别不写构图  →  AI默认居中，证件照感
❌ 主体居中 + 无引导线  →  视线一眼到底，无故事感
❌ 全部元素均匀分布  →  视觉疲劳，没有重点
❌ 只用 "rule of thirds" 不具体说明方向  →  AI理解模糊，效果随机
```
